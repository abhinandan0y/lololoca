Live Personalization Experience

Unlike traditional craft stalls, every portrait is created live in front of visitors. Customers select ceramic faces, hairstyles, outfits, skin tones, pets, accessories and Vienna-themed backgrounds while watching the artwork come to life.

Each handcrafted ceramic component is produced from scratch using natural clay, kiln firing, hand painting and detailed face drawing — making every smile truly unique.

From Clay to Portrait

100% handcrafted ceramic production process

Clay Preparation

Natural liquid clay is prepared and poured into custom plaster molds.

Kiln Firing

Each component is kiln-fired at 950°C for durability and artistic quality.

Hand Painting

Every body, face and accessory is individually hand-painted with intricate details.

Live Assembly

The final personalized portrait is assembled live in front of customers within minutes.

Sustainably Handcrafted

All ceramic components are handmade using natural clay, FSC-certified wooden frames and water-based non-toxic paints. Every portrait is produced on demand to minimize waste and eliminate mass production.
